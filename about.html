Our Story

We Built This Because the Professionals Around Us Deserved Better.

925+ did not start as a business idea. It started as a conversation about what it means to build a career that actually matters.

Where It Started

A Room Full of People Who Were Still Reaching.

In early 2026, we held our first webinar. Ninety-two professionals registered. They came from medicine, education, technology, law, media, agriculture, and everywhere in between. They were qualified. They were capable. They were showing up every day.

But when we asked what they were struggling with, the answers were striking in how similar they were. How do I go further without burning out? How do I stay relevant? How do I be seen? How do I know what is next?

These were not the questions of people who had given up. These were the questions of people who were still reaching, still building, still believing there was more. They just did not have a place to figure it out together.

925+ is that place.

What We Believe

The Convictions That Built This Community.

Your career is the starting point, not the ceiling.

Whatever you have built so far is not the limit of what is possible. It is the foundation for what comes next.

Visibility is not vanity. It is strategy.

The African professional is one of the most underrepresented stories in the world. Not because the talent is absent, but because the visibility has not been there.

Community accelerates everything.

When professionals grow together, they grow faster, further, and with far less of the loneliness that usually comes with ambition.

The next generation deserves to see what is possible.

Before they decide it is not. Before the wrong narratives take root. Before they give up on the careers that could change their lives.

Belonging should not be gatekept.

You do not need a certain title, a certain salary, or a certain level of success to belong here. You just need to have decided to be more.

Generosity is the default.

We share what we know, open the doors we have walked through, and invest in each other without keeping score.

Who Is Behind 925+

Built by Someone Who Has Been in That Room.

Dr. Testimony Olajire
Founder, 925+ & K-STEPS Technologies

925+ is founded by Dr. Testimony Olajire, CEO and founder of K-STEPS Technologies, a ghostwriter, personal branding strategist, and someone who has spent years helping African professionals become visible in the rooms that matter.

925+ grew out of a simple observation: the professionals who needed community the most were the ones least likely to find it in the places that already existed. Too formal. Too transactional. Too distant from the reality of building a career in Nigeria.

So we built something different. Warm. Industry-based. Built for action, not just conversation.

Where We Are Going

A Continent-Wide Network of Professionals Who Show Up.

925+ is building a continent-wide network of professionals who are visible, connected, and invested in each other's growth.

In the near term, that means industry chapters across healthcare, education, technology, media, law, and beyond. It means career outreach programmes in secondary schools and universities. It means health outreaches, professional development, and funding conversations.

In the long term, it means a generation of African professionals who never had to figure it out alone.
