The Community

Small Enough to Matter.
Big Enough to Move Things.

925+ is not a broadcast group. It is a place where professionals in the same field find each other, challenge each other, and grow together.

How the Community Works

Two Things at Once.

When you join 925+, you become part of two things at once. The main 925+ community, where everyone belongs and the bigger conversations happen. And your Chapter, a smaller, industry-based group of professionals in your field where the real connections are made.

This is intentional. A community of hundreds can feel anonymous. A chapter of twenty people who all work in healthcare, or education, or technology, feels like something you actually belong to.

01

The Main Community

Everyone belongs here. Announcements, webinars, community-wide wins, and the bigger conversations about professional life in Africa.

02

Your Chapter

A small, industry-based group of professionals in your field. Where the real connections happen. Where you are known, not just a member.

03

The Verticals

Initiative-based groups for members involved in Futures, Health outreaches, Visibility, and Funding programmes. Action-oriented and purpose-driven.

Inside a Chapter

What Actually Happens.

Each chapter is a WhatsApp group anchored by a Chapter Lead, a member who facilitates conversations and keeps the community active. Chapters have monthly conversations around themes that matter: visibility, career navigation, staying relevant, building in public, and more.

As chapters grow, they multiply. New leads emerge. New groups form. The community stays intimate even as it expands. This is how 925+ scales without losing what makes it valuable.

1
You register and select your chapter

Tell us your field and your interests. We connect you to the right group.

2
You join the main community first

Everyone starts in the main 925+ WhatsApp group where announcements and community conversations happen.

3
Your chapter opens and you are invited in

As chapters launch, members who selected that interest are invited into their industry group.

4
The chapter grows and multiplies

When a chapter reaches capacity, it splits. New leads emerge. The intimacy is preserved.

The Main 925+ Community

Where Everyone Belongs.

Beyond your chapter, you are part of the main 925+ community group. This is where webinar announcements go out, where community-wide wins are celebrated, where opportunities are shared, and where the bigger conversations about professional life in Africa happen.

It is also where you will hear about 925+ Futures and how to get involved in career outreaches, health programmes, and everything else the community is doing.

What We Stand For

The Values That Hold This Together.

Visibility over silence.

Your story deserves to be told. We help each other show up, stay consistent, and be seen by the people who need to find us.

Depth over noise.

We are not here for surface-level networking. We are here for real growth, real conversations, and real relationships that last.

Generosity as a default.

We share what we know, open the doors we have walked through, and invest in each other without keeping score.

Belonging without gatekeeping.

You do not need a certain title or salary to be here. You just need to have decided to be more.
