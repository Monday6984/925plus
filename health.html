925+ Health

Healthcare Professionals Who Show Up Beyond the Clinic.

Inside 925+ Health, medical professionals are taking their expertise into communities that need it most.

Beyond the Consulting Room

Your Training Was Never Meant to Stay Inside Four Walls.

Nigeria has some of the most skilled healthcare professionals in the world. Doctors, nurses, pharmacists, medical laboratory scientists, dentists, mental health practitioners, and more. Many of them trained hard, built strong careers, and show up every day in demanding conditions.

But the impact of a healthcare professional does not have to stop at the clinic door.

925+ Health is a vertical within the 925+ community where healthcare professionals come together to organise outreaches, screenings, health talks, and community interventions that take their expertise beyond their institutions and into the hands of people who would otherwise never access it.

"The impact of a healthcare professional does not have to stop at the clinic door."

What 925+ Health Does

Taking Expertise to Where It Is Needed Most.

Members of the 925+ Health chapter organise and participate in community health programmes across Nigeria. The format is flexible and driven by the professionals in the network.

Health Outreaches

Community visits where 925+ Health members offer basic screenings, consultations, and health education to underserved populations. Free. Accessible. Professional.

Health Talks

Sessions in schools, churches, community centres, and workplaces where healthcare professionals speak on topics that affect everyday Nigerians. Mental health. Maternal health. Preventive care. Chronic disease management.

Professional Panels

Conversations within the 925+ community where health professionals share knowledge across disciplines and help each other navigate the intersection of clinical work and career building.
