925+ Funding

Your Salary Is Not Your Ceiling.

925+ Funding is where professionals have honest conversations about money, income, and building financial futures that go beyond the monthly paycheck.

The Conversation Nobody Is Having

You Were Trained to Earn. Nobody Taught You to Build.

Most professionals were trained to earn. Study hard, get a good job, collect a salary, repeat. Nobody taught you how to build income streams beyond that salary. Nobody showed you how to position your expertise as something people pay for outside of your employer.

Nobody had the honest conversation about grants, investments, side businesses, and what financial independence actually looks like for someone with your background.

And so most professionals stay stuck. Not because they lack ambition. Not because they lack skill. But because they lack the information, the community, and the permission to think bigger about money.

925+ Funding is that conversation. Finally.

"Most professionals stay stuck not because they lack ambition, but because they lack the permission to think bigger about money."

What Happens Inside 925+ Funding

Four Conversations That Change How You Think About Income.

925+ Funding is built around the conversations that most professional spaces avoid. Practical, honest, and grounded in the reality of building wealth in Nigeria.

01

Income Beyond the Salary

Conversations and frameworks around building income streams that complement your career. Consulting, freelancing, monetising expertise, and building products from what you already know.

02

Grants and Opportunities

Information, guidance, and community support for professionals looking to access grants, fellowships, and funding opportunities. Many professionals do not apply because they do not know where to look or how to position themselves.

03

Investment Conversations

Honest, accessible conversations about personal finance and investment for the Nigerian professional. Not jargon. Not theory. Practical guidance from people who are figuring it out alongside you.

04

Business Building

Support for professionals who are building businesses, side hustles, or ventures alongside their careers. From idea to first client to scaling, the community shares what works and what does not.

Who 925+ Funding Is For

Any Professional Who Has Ever Felt Their Income Does Not Reflect Their Value.

Any professional who wants to build something beyond their job but does not know where to start. Any professional who has heard about grants, investments, or income diversification but never had a trusted space to explore what it means for them personally.

You do not need to already be building a business. You just need to be open to the possibility that there is more.

You have skills people pay for but you have never charged anyone outside your employer.

You have heard about grants and fellowships but never applied because you did not know how.

You have a business idea that has been sitting in your head for longer than you want to admit.

You want to invest but do not know where to start or who to trust for advice.

Your salary feels like it should be the floor, not the ceiling, of what you earn.

The Community Advantage

You Do Not Have to Figure This Out Alone.

The most valuable thing about 925+ Funding is not any single piece of information. It is the community around it. Professionals who have navigated the same questions. People who have applied for grants and can tell you exactly how.

Entrepreneurs who built their first income stream while still in a full-time job and can show you the path. When you are in a room with people who are a few steps ahead of where you are, the distance between where you are and where you want to be shrinks dramatically.
