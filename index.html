Why 925+ Exists

You Are Doing the Work.
But Nobody Sees It.

You are qualified. You are capable. You show up every single day. But somewhere between the demands of the job, the weight of life, and the noise of everything else, your story is not being told.

Your expertise is not reaching the people it should. And the version of your career that could really matter is still waiting.

You are not alone in that. Ninety-two professionals registered for our first webinar. They came from medicine, education, technology, law, media, engineering, and everywhere in between. And almost all of them asked the same kinds of questions.

  • "How do I go beyond my 9 to 5 without burning out?"
  • "How do I stay relevant when everything is changing so fast?"
  • "How do I show up and be seen without losing myself in the process?"
  • "How do I know when it is time to build something more for myself?"
  • "Is it wise to stay on just one niche in the 21st century?"
What is 925+

More Than a Community.
A Decision.

925+ is what happens when professionals stop waiting for permission to be more than their job title. It is a growing network of working professionals across different fields in Nigeria and beyond, connected by one shared belief.

Your career is the starting point, not the ceiling.

We run webinars, industry-based chapters, and outreach programmes. We help each other stay visible, stay relevant, and stay human in the middle of demanding careers. And we do it together, because the journey is better that way.

"More than the job. More than enough."
